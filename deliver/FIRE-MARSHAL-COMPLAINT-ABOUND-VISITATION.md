## WASHINGTON BUILDING CODE VIOLATIONS

### Chapter 10 - Means of Egress

**Section 1006.2.1 - Two Egress Doors Required:**
> "Where a building contains two or more occupancies, the means of egress requirements apply to each portion based on the occupancy of that space."

**Violation:** Facility lacks two visible egress systems on 2nd floor.

**Section 1029.1 - Emergency Escape and Rescue Openings:**
> "Basements, habitable attics and every sleeping room shall have not fewer than one operable emergency escape and rescue opening."

**Violation:** No emergency rescue openings identified or posted.

**Section 403.5 - Fire Safety and Evacuation Plans:**
> "Fire safety and evacuation plans shall be provided for the following occupancies... Group E occupancies with an occupant load of 100 or more persons above or below the lowest level of exit discharge."

**Violation:** No fire safety or evacuation plan posted.
