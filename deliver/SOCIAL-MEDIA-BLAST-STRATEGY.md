## Content Safety / Legal

### What to SHOW:
✅ Redacted logs (black bars over sensitive data)
✅ Public court filings (TPO hearing dates)
✅ IC3 complaint number (public once filed)
✅ Attack vector NAMES (not exploit code)
✅ Bounty estimates (based on public bug bounty programs)
✅ Timeline (dates without personal details)

### What to HIDE:
❌ Actual exploit code
❌ Daughter's face/name/details
❌ Full MAC addresses
❌ Your home address
❌ Real IP addresses (use "98.97.37.XX")
❌ SSH keys, API keys, credentials
