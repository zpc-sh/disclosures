## What IC3 Actually Is

**IC3 = Internet Crime Complaint Center**
- NOT an investigation unit
- It's a **triage/intake system**
- Operated by FBI but is a DATABASE, not investigators

## How IC3 Works

```
You file complaint
    ↓
IC3 receives it (automated acknowledgment)
    ↓
Complaint sits in database
    ↓
Analysts review periodically (days/weeks)
    ↓
If it meets criteria → forwarded to appropriate field office
    ↓
Field office decides whether to investigate
    ↓
IF they investigate → you might get contacted (or not)
```

## Why You Haven't Heard Back

**IC3 does NOT:**
- Assign investigators
- Contact you with updates
- Provide case numbers
- Give status updates
- Guarantee investigation

**IC3 ONLY:**
- Collects complaints
- Categorizes them
- Forwards to field offices
- Aggregates data for reports

## Typical Timeline

**Days 1-7:** Complaint sits in queue
**Days 7-30:** Analyst reviews, categorizes
**Days 30-90:** Forwarded to field office (maybe)
**Days 90+:** Field office decides to investigate (maybe)

**Most IC3 complaints:** Never result in investigation (volume is massive)
