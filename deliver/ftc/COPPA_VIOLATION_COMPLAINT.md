## Google's COPPA Obligations

### Requirements Under COPPA

**For services with child users, Google must:**
1. Obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal info
2. Provide parents notice of collection practices
3. Limit collection to what's necessary
4. Protect confidentiality and security of child's info
5. Delete child's personal info upon parent's request

### Failures in This Case

1. **Security:** Gemini vulnerabilities allowed unauthorized access to child's photos
2. **Protection:** No special safeguards for COPPA-protected content
3. **Notice:** No alert to parent about Photos access
4. **Confidentiality:** Child's images compromised by third party
